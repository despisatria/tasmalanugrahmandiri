About Us

CV. Tasmal Anugrah Mandiri is a Bandung-based company founded in 2012 that provides comprehensive maintenance and repair services for healthcare and industrial equipment. We are a trusted partner for our clients, offering a combination of experienced technicians, competitive pricing, and a commitment to quality.

  • Our Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a trusted partner for customers in healthcare and mechanical electrical equipment maintenance and repair services in Indonesia, prioritizing safety, reliability, efficiency, and innovation, thereby providing added value to customers and the general public.

Mission

  • To be a trusted partner that provides outstanding service for customer satisfaction, prioritizing safety, reliability, and efficiency.
  • To build mutually beneficial relationships with customers, suppliers, and employees, by creating a positive and productive work environment.
  • To develop and implement the latest technologies to improve service quality and operational efficiency.
  • To continuously improve product and service quality
  • To make a positive contribution to society and the environment, by supporting social and environmental programs.
